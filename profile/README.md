## About RESC — Renewable Energy & Smart Control

**RESC** (Renewable Energy & Smart Control) is a Saudi Arabian technology company headquartered in **Saudi Arabia**, specializing in **solar energy systems**, **power electronics**, and **industrial automation**. We design and develop end-to-end hardware, firmware, and software solutions that power the next generation of smart energy infrastructure across the Kingdom.

Our interdisciplinary engineering team delivers complete solutions — from PCB-level embedded firmware to Flutter mobile applications — enabling real-time monitoring, control, and diagnostics of energy systems in the field.

## Our Products

### 🔲 CBX Series — Smart Combiner Box Controllers

The **CBX Series** is RESC's flagship product line for solar PV combiner box control. Designed for Saudi Arabian and GCC solar farms, the CBX integrates string-level current monitoring, DC protection, remote diagnostics, and Modbus communication into a compact DIN-rail ready unit.

- **[CBX-Controller-FW](https://github.com/RESC-SA/CBX-Controller-FW)** — Main embedded firmware (C) for the CBX controller board
- **[CBX-Interface-Board-FW](https://github.com/RESC-SA/CBX-Interface-Board-FW)** — HMI and RS485/Modbus communication interface firmware
- **[Combiner Module (C)](https://github.com/RESC-SA/Combiner_module_C)** — Reusable C library for power measurement, string protection logic, and fault detection

### 📱 Inverter Gateway — Flutter Mobile App

The **Inverter Gateway** is a Bluetooth-enabled mobile application for solar inverter commissioning and diagnostics. Field engineers can configure, monitor, and troubleshoot inverters on-site using Modbus RTU over BLE — without needing a laptop or physical serial cable.

**Built with:** Flutter · Dart · Modbus RTU · Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)
